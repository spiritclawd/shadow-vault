# Shadow Vault — Business Model

## The Problem Worth Solving

AI agents are trading **$4B+ daily** on Solana alone. Every single trade is visible. 
Every position is copyable. Every strategy is extractable by MEV bots.

The agent economy needs privacy infrastructure — and nobody is selling it to them.

---

## Revenue Streams

### 1. Transaction Fees (Protocol Revenue)
**Model:** 0.1% on every deposit + withdraw through Shadow Vault
**Example:** Agent deposits $10M/month → $10K/month protocol revenue
**At scale:** 1,000 agents × $5M avg volume = **$5M/month**

### 2. Agent SDK Pro (SaaS)
**Free tier:** Basic vault (init, deposit, withdraw)
**Pro tier ($99/mo):** 
- Policy engine (spend limits, market restrictions)
- Encrypted audit logs
- Multi-agent vault sharing
- Priority execution

**Enterprise tier ($999/mo):**
- Custom policies
- Compliance exports
- Dedicated support
- White-label SDK

### 3. Privacy-as-a-Service (API)
**Model:** Per-call pricing for protocols integrating Shadow Vault
**Target:** DEX aggregators, trading bots, DeFi protocols
**Pricing:** $0.005 per encrypted operation
**At scale:** 10M operations/day = **$50K/day**

### 4. Data & Analytics (Opt-in)
**Model:** Anonymized aggregate trading data sold to:
- Market makers (flow analysis)
- Research firms (agent behavior patterns)
- Protocols (product development)
**Revenue share:** 50% to vault owners, 50% to protocol

### 5. Governance Token (Long-term)
**Ticker:** $SHADOW
**Utility:**
- Fee discounts
- Governance votes (policy templates, fee rates)
- Staking for premium features
- Revenue sharing from protocol fees
